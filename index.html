Welcome to Nikko Gurung Photography

Hey there, I’m Nikko, an adventurous soul with an insatiable curiosity for the world around me. My friends tell me I see the world as my playground, willing to step outside my comfort zone, enriching my own life story and also encouraging those around me to explore and discover.

From scaling mountain peaks to wandering through vibrant city streets, I believe that every day is an opportunity for a new adventure. My passion for exploration drives me to seek out unique experiences, whether it’s hiking through snow-capped mountains, cycling into crystal-clear waters, or immersing myself in different cultures.

Join me as I navigate your unique journey. Let’s embrace the unknown together, celebrate the beauty of your special day, and inspire each other to live life to the fullest! Adventure awaits—let’s dive in!
